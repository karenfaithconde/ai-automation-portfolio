# Sweet Bites Order Automation

## What it does
Automatically captures cake orders from Google Forms, saves 
them to Google Sheets, and sends a professional confirmation 
email to every customer — no manual work needed.

## Tools used
- Make.com
- Google Forms
- Google Sheets
- Gmail

## Real world use case
A small bakery receives custom cake orders through a form. 
Every order is automatically saved and the customer instantly 
receives a confirmation email with their order details —
without the owner doing anything manually.

## How it works
1. Customer submits a cake order form
2. Make captures the order instantly
3. Order is saved to Google Sheets
4. Customer receives a personalized confirmation email 
   with their name, cake flavor, and delivery date
