# Lead Capture Automation

## What it does
Automatically captures form submissions and saves them 
to a Google Sheet in real time — no manual data entry needed.

## Tools used
- Make.com
- Google Forms
- Google Sheets

## Real world use case
A business owner receives leads from their website contact 
form. Every lead is automatically organized in a spreadsheet,
timestamped and ready to follow up.

## How it works
1. Someone submits the Google Form
2. Make detects the new response within 15 minutes
3. Make automatically writes the data to Google Sheets
