# Lead Capture Automation

## What it does
Captures Google Form submissions, saves them to Google Sheets, 
and sends an automatic personalized confirmation email to 
every person who fills out the form — no manual work needed.

## Tools used
- Make.com
- Google Forms
- Google Sheets
- Gmail

## Real world use case
A business owner receives leads from their website contact 
form. Every lead is automatically saved to a spreadsheet 
and instantly receives a personalized confirmation email — 
without the owner doing anything manually.

## How it works
1. Someone submits the Google Form
2. Make detects the new response
3. Make saves the data to Google Sheets
4. Make automatically sends a personalized confirmation 
   email to the submitter
